Matka, Matka, Matka!

FoodDrinksMeals

Matka Live Kitchen, located in Tangail, Bangladesh, offers a diverse range of dishes. My cousins and I grabbed some hot wings, chicken momos, chicken chowmein, and a simple chicken burger. However, they're popular for their "Matka Tea."

Offered in a cute, tiny pot, this tea was served hot! Embellished with toppings and mixed with honey, this drink was perfect to sip on a breezy, winter evening.
