Home Cooked Meals

FoodMealsHomeCelebrations

As I get older, I recognize the importance of home cooked meals. That is, appreciating my mother's meals that she works so hard to cook as a working mom. That is when she doesn't bring home take out that I try not to eat because of how unhealthy it could be.

So, this is my official transition into posting homecooked meals that I create. I've been getting into cooking and baking a lot this past summer. It's been fun. I'll post them after this post.

I'm lucky that I had the opportunity and free time to prepare these dishes. They've all been a success, thankfully.

Baking and cooking is so cool because you get to create something with love and share it with others. It should be a distinct love language. I got all the recipes from ChatGPT, which helped me personalize the dishes to my own taste. Hope it inspires others to get into baking/cooking, if they wish. Enjoy!
