Strawberry milkshake

FoodDessertDrinksNYC EatsCelebrations

You can't get girlier than drinking a strawberry milkshake in the summertime. My aunt and cousin, who lives in Bangladesh, helped me make this. Thank you khalamonin and Arisha muni!

In this blog post, I will share the recipe my relatives have passed down to me.

Strawberry milkshake recipe:

- Fill half the blender with water

- Cut 10 strawberries into small slices

- 2 tbsp Dano dry whole milk

- 2 tbsp sweetened condensed milk

- Blend

Enjoy!
